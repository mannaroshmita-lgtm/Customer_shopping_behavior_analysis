# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis | End-to-End Data Analytics Project


> **An End-to-End Data Analytics Project using Python, MySQL, and Power BI to analyze customer shopping behavior and generate actionable business insights.**

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Customer shopping behavior plays a vital role in helping retail businesses understand purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and revenue drivers.

This project analyzes **3,900 customer purchase records** to uncover meaningful insights into customer demographics, purchasing habits, subscription behavior, product performance, and sales trends. The project demonstrates a complete data analytics workflow—from data cleaning and preprocessing to SQL-based business analysis and interactive dashboard development.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to transform raw retail transaction data into meaningful business insights that support better decision-making.

The analysis answers questions such as:

- Which customer groups contribute the highest revenue?
- How do discounts affect purchasing behavior?
- Are subscribers more valuable than non-subscribers?
- Which products receive the highest customer ratings?
- Which age groups generate the most revenue?
- How can customer loyalty and retention be improved?

# 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing (Python)

The dataset was cleaned and prepared using **Python (Pandas)** in Google Colab.

### Steps Performed

- Imported the dataset using Pandas
- Performed initial data exploration
- Checked missing values
- Imputed missing values in the **Review Rating** column using the median rating of each product category
- Standardized column names using **snake_case**
- Created **Age Group** feature
- Created **Purchase Frequency** feature
- Removed redundant columns after consistency checks
- Exported the cleaned dataset for SQL analysis

 # 📈 Key Insights

After analyzing 3,900 customer purchase records, the following insights were identified:

- Revenue generation varies across customer demographics, with certain age groups contributing a significantly larger share of total sales.
- Customers who subscribe tend to generate higher overall revenue, indicating strong potential for retention-focused marketing strategies.
- Several products maintain high customer ratings, making them ideal candidates for promotional campaigns.
- Discount strategies influence purchasing behavior, but some products rely heavily on discounts, which may reduce overall profit margins.
- Repeat customers represent a valuable customer segment and can be converted into loyal customers through targeted reward programs.
- Shipping preferences influence purchase value, with express shipping customers showing different spending patterns compared to standard shipping users.

---

# 📢 Business Recommendations


Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:

- Increase subscriber engagement by offering exclusive discounts, early access to products, and loyalty rewards.
- Introduce customer loyalty programs to encourage repeat purchases and improve customer retention.
- Review discount policies for products that heavily depend on promotional offers to maintain healthy profit margins.
- Highlight top-rated and best-selling products in marketing campaigns to maximize conversions.
- Focus personalized marketing campaigns on high-value customer segments identified during the analysis.
- Improve inventory planning by monitoring product demand across different categories and seasons.
